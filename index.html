1. Overview

LushLyrics is an Android app that shows synced lyrics for songs you play through Spotify. The app uses the official Spotify Web API to read your playback information and Google AdMob to display ads.

This policy explains what information is processed, how it is used, and your choices. By using LushLyrics, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information processed through Spotify

When you connect LushLyrics to your Spotify account, you authorize the app to access certain data via the Spotify Web API. This typically includes:

  • Currently playing track (title, artist, album, duration)
  • Playback status (play, pause, position in track)
  • Your playback device information (for syncing and display)
  • Optionally, your playlists and queue to show upcoming tracks in the UI

LushLyrics uses this information only to:

  • Display synced lyrics in real time
  • Show which song is playing, and what is next in your queue or playlist
  • Keep the floating lyrics overlay in sync with your music

LushLyrics does not store your Spotify password. Authentication is handled by Spotify, and the app receives an access token and refresh token as part of the standard OAuth process.

3. Tokens and local storage

To keep you signed in, LushLyrics may store your Spotify access token and refresh token securely on your device. These tokens are used only to communicate with the Spotify Web API and are not uploaded to any separate server controlled by the developer.

If you disconnect LushLyrics from your Spotify account (for example by logging out inside the app or revoking access in your Spotify account settings), the tokens will no longer be usable.

4. Google AdMob and advertising

LushLyrics uses Google AdMob to show ads inside the app. AdMob may collect and process information independently of the developer, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • Approximate location (for regional ad relevance)
  • App interaction data (for example, which screens are visited)
  • Information about how often ads are shown and clicked

This information is handled according to Google’s own privacy policy and AdMob terms. You can manage your ad preferences, including limiting personalized ads, through your device settings or your Google account settings.

5. Permissions used by the app

LushLyrics may request certain Android permissions to provide its core features, for example:

  • Internet access: required to communicate with Spotify and AdMob.
  • Overlay / draw over other apps: used to show the floating lyrics bubble or overlay on top of other apps while music is playing.
  • Notifications: if enabled, used to keep the lyrics service running in the foreground and show playback/lyrics status.

These permissions are used only for the purposes described and are not used to read your personal content outside of what is necessary for lyrics and playback display.

6. Data storage and retention

LushLyrics does not run its own remote backend for storing your personal data. Most information is processed in real time and kept only on your device while you use the app.

Locally stored items may include:

  • Spotify access and refresh tokens
  • Basic app preferences (for example, theme, overlay settings)

You can remove these by uninstalling the app or clearing its data in your device settings.

7. Data sharing

LushLyrics does not sell your personal data.

Data may be shared with:

  • Spotify: as required to provide playback and lyrics syncing through the official Spotify Web API.
  • Google AdMob: for showing ads and measuring their performance.

These services operate under their own privacy policies. You can review them on Spotify’s and Google’s official websites.

8. Children’s privacy

LushLyrics is not directed at children under 13 and is intended for general audiences who use Spotify. If you are under the minimum age in your region to create a Spotify account or to consent to data processing, you should not use LushLyrics without a parent or guardian.

9. Your choices and controls

You can control your data and experience in several ways:

  • Disconnect LushLyrics from Spotify by revoking its access in your Spotify account settings or logging out in the app.
  • Manage ad personalization and advertising ID settings in your Android system settings and Google account.
  • Remove app data by uninstalling LushLyrics or clearing its storage in your device settings.

10. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the app’s features or data handling change. When that happens, the “Last updated” date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
